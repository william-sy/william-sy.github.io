Swiss roll - recipe 1

Ingredients:

  • 5 whole eggs
  • 50 gram whole milk
  • 50 gram vegetable oil
  • 2 gram baking powder
  • 90 cake flour
  • 120 gram granulated sugar
  • 1 gram salt

Steps:

Yolks:

  • Seperate egg yolks from whites
  • Add 1 gram of salt to yolks
  • Gently stir mixture
  • Add 50 gramm of sugar into mixture
  • Mix until smooth and foamy

Whites:

  • Use a mixer to mix well
  • Add 70 gram of sugar in 3 batches
  • Mix until stiff peaks

Batter:

  • Add egg yolks to whites and fold until integrated
  • Add 90 grams of cake flour (through sieve) and fold until smooth
  • Take a scoop and mix it seperately with 50 grams of milk and 50 gramms of oil
  • Add mixture back into egg yolk mixture
  • Pour the mixture into a rectangle cake pan, and even out.
  • Slam it on the counter to remove bubbles

Baking:

  • Bake in preheated oven at 170 celsius for 30 minutes
  • Slam on counter and remove cake out of baking pan
  • Place a baking paper or mat on top of the cake and invert it over

Finshing touches:

  • Trim the sides of the cake to get a rectangle
  • Cut one end with a 45 degree corner
  • Cut a few lines 3/4 through the cake to ease follding of cake
  • Spread jam / Sirup / Whipcream on the cake
  • Slowly roll it into a roll wrapped with the baking paper
  • Let it rest for 1 hour in fridge before serving

Enjoy!
